Programs

Sleep Better Feel Better

Sleep better Feel better offers simple yet effective practices to decrease the levels of stress hormones that can disrupt your ability to transition into sleep and remain asleep. Learn valuable internal listening techniques to release mental and physical tensions and restore balance throughout your entire system. Discover how guided movement with synchronized breathing, restorative yoga and breath practices can cultivate sensory awareness throughout your body and begin to unlock the keys to sleeping and feeling better.

Intelligent Evolution

This Somatic journey will be using awareness through movement lessons from the Feldenkrais® Method to retrace and clear primary movement pathways. This series of Developmental lessons offers building blocks for learning to restore healthy connections and naturally diminish limitations and underlying physical and psychological issues.

Discover ways to open the vital centers improving the body’s natural ability to move towards pleasure, resiliency and wholeness, while creating more freedom and greater awareness throughout your life.

Weekly Classes

Each month a series of classes will be offered at the East West Yoga Studio in La Jolla Village. Individual adjustments and classes tailored to meet your personal needs are a part of this deeply restorative experience. Each month functional themes to optimize your awareness of health are presented with handouts to support your home practice and deepen your understanding of the Feldenkrais® Method. Adjustments are offered throughout the class to access openings that are not available without hands-on support. Experience a better flow of energy and a delicious state of relaxed awareness that will stream into your life. Restore your natural rhythms and support your flexibility, strength, balance and vitality.

Private Sessions

Functional Integration (FI) is a hands-on approach to human development and the improvement of functioning through passive movement and directed touch. Learning and change are achieved through subtle skilled manipulation that supports the nervous systems’ ability to self-organize towards more effective and intelligent action. This gentle approach is recognized for its versatility to meet a wide range of needs and enrich the quality of movement throughout your life.
